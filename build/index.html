What is Staking?



Staking is similar to a Certificate of Deposit (CD) at your local bank.

Your choice of 3 deposit terms are offered: Weekly, Monthly or Quarterly. Monthly is rewarded at 1.5 times the weekly rate, and quarterly at twice the weekly rate.

There are no minimum stakes, and you can withdraw at any time.
